Nicolas Bray's CSCI 1301

CSCI 1301 - Introduction to Computing and Programming

This was the first coding course I took in college, back when I thought I wanted to be a CompSci minor and before I'd even heard about NMI. This course helped to lay the foundation of my technical/coding knowledge and has made tackling new concepts in NMI courses more comfortable. Completing this course helped lead me to NMIX and EMST as I knew that I wanted to stay in the technical world, but that CompSci wasn't the right path for me. The NMI offered instruction on a really cool blend of computational and media skills which bridged the gap between my old major, CompSci, and my new major, EMST.
